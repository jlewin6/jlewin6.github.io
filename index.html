Understanding the Relationships
Between Clinical Trials

Welcome to team 38s interactive exploration tool of the clinical trial data. Hundreds of thousands of clinical trials are registered on ClinicalTrials.gov, which provides a static representation of trial data and does not show relationships between them.

We propose to develop an innovative and user-friendly visualization tool to help users navigate the trials in a visual format. We hope to help excelerate the speed at which doctors, patients, and others can find relavant clinical trial data for their research purposes.
